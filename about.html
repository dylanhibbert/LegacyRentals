about

get to know us

Legacy Rentals brings a new meaning to short term rentals. Service, design and comfort are at our core; when you book a stay with us, you’re guaranteed to have an experience that will leave a legacy. From the moment you walk in the door, you’ll feel the Legacy hospitality. Inspired by the surrounding landscape, Legacy Rentals was designed to make your visit a unique, comfortable and fun experience. Our rooms are spacious , our fares are competitive and the service is unparalleled.

Our team knows that traveling can be exhausting. Therefore, we offer 24/7 customer support via text or phone to ensure any questions, concerns or accommodations are addressed. Our team ensures that we provide quality service to not only meet the needs of our customers, but to also make their stay one that is memorable!

Looking to stay with us for a month or longer? We welcome longer stays too. Our company offers 30+ night stays to accommodate the business traveler, long-haul vacationer, and destination wanderer. Located in Las Vegas, we offer travelers extended stay accommodations with our fully-furnished apartment suites that seamlessly integrate work and play while providing all the comforts of home.

Feel free to browse our site for more information regarding stays and amenities. We look forward to welcoming you soon!

FAQs

Does Legacy Rentals screen their guests?​​​​​​

Yes! No matter the length of the stay, we require all identity verification and screening before booking.​


How does Legacy Rentals market its stays?​

We advertise our rentals here on our direct booking website. In addition, we also market them on other third-party short-term rental sites.​


Who are Legacy Rentals guests, and how long are their typical stays?

Our Legacy guests include those traveling for leisure, business, or corporate stays. Our stays range from one night to three months.


​

Does Legacy Rentals have a cleaning team?​

After each stay, our professional Legacy cleaning team will inspect and clean each unit. If a guest books for an extended stay, we clean our space weekly upon request.​
